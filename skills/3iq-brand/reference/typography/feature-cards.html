Our digital asset solutions

We offer a range of passive investment solutions designed to provide exposure to the dynamic world of digital assets. Our product suite is tailored to meet diverse investor needs and risk profiles:

Single asset exposure

Gain focused exposure to leading individual cryptocurrencies through our carefully structured investment vehicles. These products offer a secure and regulated way to invest in specific digital assets, allowing you to:

  • Access major cryptocurrencies through familiar investment structures
  • Benefit from our institutional-grade security and custody solutions
  • Enjoy the simplicity of investing without the complexity of direct cryptocurrency ownership

Multi-asset funds

For those seeking broader exposure, our diversified funds provide access to a curated selection of digital assets. These products offer a balanced approach to investing in the cryptocurrency market, enabling you to:

  • Invest in a diversified portfolio of digital assets with a single investment
  • Benefit from our expert selection and ongoing portfolio management
  • Potentially reduce volatility through diversification across multiple digital assets
